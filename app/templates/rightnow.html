What To Do When You Need Legal Help

1

Assess Your Situation

Identify what type of legal issue you're facing - is it criminal, civil, labor, property, or family law related?

2

Know Your Rights

Understand what rights you have in this situation. Use our rights library to learn about applicable laws and constitutional protections.

3

Document Everything

Collect evidence: dates, times, witnesses, photographs, messages, or any relevant documents related to your case.

4

Send Legal Notice

If applicable, send a formal legal notice to the other party. Use our document generator for professionally drafted notices.

5

Seek Legal Help

Contact a lawyer, legal aid service, or ombudsman. Many services are free for low-income individuals.
