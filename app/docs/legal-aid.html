Legal Aid Locator

Access free legal services provided by the Government of India for those in need.

NALSA

National Legal Services Authority

The primary body responsible for providing free legal services to the weaker sections of society and organizing Lok Adalats for amicable settlement of disputes.

Visit NALSA Website

Who is Eligible?

Free legal services are available to:

  • • Women and Children
  • • Members of SC/ST
  • • Industrial Workmen
  • • Persons in custody
  • • Persons with annual income below mandated limit

State Authorities

Every state has a State Legal Services Authority (SLSA). You can find your specific state's portal here:

  • Delhi State LSA
  • Maharashtra State LSA
  • Karnataka State LSA
  • Uttar Pradesh State LSA
